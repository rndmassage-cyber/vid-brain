# Pizza, Cheese Pull and Baked Foods

## Core Thesis
Baked foods sell the contrast between a cooked outer surface and a hot, yielding interior.  
For pizza in particular, tension lives between crust structure and molten elasticity.

## Physical Identity of the Food
Key traits:
- browned or blistered exterior
- visible bake marks or crust character
- high heat cues
- stretch or tear potential
- clean distinction between crust, topping, sauce, and melt

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Preserve crust definition.
- Maintain a boundary between dough, sauce, and cheese.
- If a slice lifts, the remaining pie must show the matching gap.
- Melt should feel heavy and structural, not watery.
- Toppings should not sink into an unreadable blanket.

## Best Shot Types
- blistering macro
- wheel cut or sharp slice reveal
- cheese pull lift
- steam or heat shimmer reveal
- overhead whole pie recognition
- hero slice with visible crust, sauce, and melt

## Best Camera Angles
- overhead for layout and cut logic
- 30° to 45° for appetite access
- low macro side angle for crust and pull behavior

## Best Lighting Traits
- hard or semi-hard highlights for crust texture
- backlight for steam and cheese translucency
- warm highlights to reinforce oven heat

## Best Interactions
- wheel cut
- slice lift
- crust crack
- cheese strand separation
- dipping edge into sauce
- topping movement under tension

## What Makes It Look Expensive
- leopard spotting or intelligent crust texture
- thick luminous melt
- clear interior-exterior contrast
- visible heat
- controlled topping distribution
- a strong lifted-slice payoff

## What Makes It Look Cheap
- flat pale crust
- toppings buried in generic yellow cheese
- no slice-gap aftermath
- cheese behaving like thin plastic strings
- messy topping slide with no structure

## What Must Stay Consistent
- topping map family
- crust thickness and char logic
- sauce boundary
- cheese color and density
- slice geometry after cutting

## Common Failure Modes in Generative Video
- cut lines appear and disappear
- a lifted slice leaves no missing space
- cutter warps or melts into food
- toppings teleport to other zones
- cheese fuses into the crust with no boundary
