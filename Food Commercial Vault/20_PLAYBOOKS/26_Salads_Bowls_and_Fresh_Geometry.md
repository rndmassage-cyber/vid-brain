# Salads, Bowls and Fresh Geometry

## Core Thesis
Fresh bowls and salads are sold through crisp organization, hydration, and color clarity.

## Physical Identity of the Food
Key traits:
- fresh edges
- hydrated surfaces
- visible ingredient separation
- bright controlled palette
- bowl geometry or arranged plating
- low tolerance for muddiness

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Keep ingredient categories readable.
- Preserve crisp leaf or slice edges.
- Moisture must look refreshing, not soggy.
- Bowl structure should support visual order.
- Dressing should increase appetite without destroying geometry too early.

## Best Shot Types
- overhead organization shot
- fork or chopstick lift of a balanced bite
- dressing drizzle
- macro dew or fresh-cut detail
- bowl turn for color blocking
- final hero with clean freshness cues

## Best Camera Angles
- overhead for arrangement
- 30° to 45° for eating access
- macro close-up for hydration and fresh edges

## Best Lighting Traits
- clean bright-but-dimensional light
- crisp reflections on moist ingredients
- color separation that keeps greens alive
- enough shadow to preserve depth in bowls

## Best Interactions
- dressing drizzle
- fork lift
- light toss
- spoon scoop in grain or poke bowls
- garnish placement
- citrus squeeze

## What Makes It Look Expensive
- color-blocked clarity
- fresh moisture logic
- clean vessel choice
- ingredient contrast without clutter
- an orderly composition that can gracefully break during interaction

## What Makes It Look Cheap
- wilted edges
- muddy dressing coverage
- greens turning yellow
- no distinction between diced ingredients
- bowl overstuffed until the food loses shape

## What Must Stay Consistent
- ingredient zones or family layout
- bowl identity
- avocado or protein arrangement
- moisture level family
- dressing amount after drizzle

## Common Failure Modes in Generative Video
- leaves melt into a green paste
- cubes become rounded blobs
- dressing appears without source logic
- geometric arrangement changes between cuts
- ingredients lose freshness color balance
