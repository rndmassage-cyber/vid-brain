# Wraps, Tacos, Burritos and Folded Foods

## Core Thesis
Folded foods are about contained abundance.  
The appeal comes from the tension between structure and overflow.

## Physical Identity of the Food
Key traits:
- shell or wrap geometry
- packed fillings
- contrasting textures and colors
- pressure held within a folded boundary
- cross-section potential

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Folds must remain geometrically logical.
- Fillings should feel abundant but not chaotic.
- Color zones should stay separated enough to read.
- A cut wrap should reveal intentional internal organization.
- Shell fracture or tortilla bend must match material type.

## Best Shot Types
- cross-section reveal
- shell snap or bite path
- lime squeeze over the filling
- sauce drip onto packed surface
- hand-held hero reveal
- final hero with visible fill density

## Best Camera Angles
- 30° to 45° for appetite access
- side angle for cross-section
- macro for shell texture, char marks, or filling sheen

## Best Lighting Traits
- strong texture light on shells and char
- crisp highlights on wet fillings
- high color separation between ingredients

## Best Interactions
- lime squeeze
- fold reveal
- bite setup
- wrap cut
- shell break
- topping fall or garnish rain

## What Makes It Look Expensive
- distinct filling zones
- believable shell texture
- vibrant but controlled color contrast
- dense, appetizing cross-section
- moisture on fillings without muddy blending

## What Makes It Look Cheap
- muddy ingredient mix
- shell that looks rubbery and undefined
- overfilled mess with no structure
- sour cream or sauce losing its identity into the filling
- folds that do not make physical sense

## What Must Stay Consistent
- fill order family
- shell or tortilla texture
- char mark logic
- sauce placement
- cross-section geometry after cutting

## Common Failure Modes in Generative Video
- shell merges with cheese or meat
- ingredient cubes become shapeless blobs
- sour cream changes color without mixing
- the wrap cut shows different contents in the next shot
- folds teleport or reverse direction
